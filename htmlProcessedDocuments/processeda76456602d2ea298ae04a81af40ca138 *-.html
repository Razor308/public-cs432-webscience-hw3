Languishing forlornly in the middle of a golf course of all places, the once-magnificent Dunnington Mansion, also known as Poplar Hill, near Farmville, Virginia is in a sorry state. Guarded by security 24/7, acclaimed urbex photographer Bryan Sansivero was granted access to capture the property in all its faded grandeur.
