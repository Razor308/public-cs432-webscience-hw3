Missing 92-year-old Houston woman with dementia last seen Friday evening

HOUSTON, Texas (KTRK) -- Police are asking the public's help to find Yaoping Wang, 92, who suffers from dementia and went missing on Friday in Chinatown in southwest Houston.

She was last seen leaving the 9400 block of Bellaire Boulevard, just off Beltway 8, in an unknown direction around 5 p.m., wearing a yellow sweatshirt, dark pants and gray or black slippers, according to officers.
