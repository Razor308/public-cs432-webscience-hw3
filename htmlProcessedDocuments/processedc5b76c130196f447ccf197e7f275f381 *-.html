Man charged with murder after woman’s body found in Virginia Beach

VIRGINIA BEACH, Va. (WAVY) — A Virginia Beach man has been charged with murder after a woman was found dead Thursday night.

35-year-old Hagen Lawrence Roberts was detained shortly after police found 63-year-old Cynthia Sue Capp’s body in the 1200 block of Green Cedar Lane around 7:45 p.m. Police say both were listed as residents of the same address.

Police originally labeled the case a suspicious death, but later took out the murder charge against Roberts.

No other details in the case were released Friday morning, but police said it was still under active investigation.
