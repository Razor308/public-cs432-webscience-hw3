Love Yourself 承 'Her' K-Pop · 2017

Editors’ Notes On their most complex work to date, BTS deliver emotionally driven electro-pop/hip-hop hybrids in a bid for global appeal. "DNA" announces itself with seismic synthesizers, while "Best of Me" (featuring production by The Chainsmokers) drips with tender melancholy. But it's the use of gender-neutral pronouns in "Intro: Serendipity" and the discussion of youth hopelessness in "Go Go" that help make Love Yourself the band's most thought-provoking creation yet.
