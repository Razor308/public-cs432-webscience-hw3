Marnie Stern (born March 18, 1976) is an American musician, singer-songwriter, and guitarist. She has garnered acclaim for her technical skill and tapping style of guitar play.[1][2] Stern is also a current member of The 8G Band from the show Late Night with Seth Meyers.

Backed by bass player Nithin Kalvakota and former Tera Melos drummer Vincent Rogers, Stern spent most of 2010 and 2011 touring North America, Europe, and Australia in support of her self-titled third album. Highlights include several winter festivals in Europe as well as support dates with the Flaming Lips. She was chosen by Les Savy Fav to perform at the ATP Nightmare Before Christmas festival that they co-curated in December 2011 in Minehead, England.[9] In 2011, Stern was named one of Elle Magazines "12 Greatest Female Electric Guitarists",[10] and was chosen as a voter on a panel of top guitarists and other experts for the Rolling Stone Magazine "100 Greatest Guitarists List".[11] In 2012, she was named No. 87 on the Spin Magazine list of "100 Greatest Guitarists of all Time".[12]
