    UPDATE: TOU Investigation

    Updated: 10/10/2020, 4:09 p.m.

    A 26-year-old man was taken into custody and transported to a local hospital where he is being evaluated. No further information is available at this time. Investigation remains ongoing.

    Original Release:

    On October 10, 2020, approximately 2:03 p.m., Newport News police responded to the first block of Cedarwood Drive in reference to checking the welfare of an individual.

    Upon arrival, officers made contact with an adult female outside a residence who advised an adult male known to her was inside the residence. Officers attempted to make contact with the male, and he discharged a firearm inside the residence. No officers were injured, and no officers discharged a firearm.

    A perimeter was set up outside the residence, and the NNPD Tactical Operations Unit was requested to assist.

    No further information is available at this time. The investigation remains ongoing.
