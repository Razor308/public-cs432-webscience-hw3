SANIBEL, Fla. – The Clinic for the Rehabilitation of Wildlife in Sanibel is currently rehabilitating three species of squirrel native to Southwest Florida.

The CROW Clinic said in a Facebook post they are currently caring for the eastern gray squirrel, the mangrove fox squirrel, and the southern flying squirrel.
