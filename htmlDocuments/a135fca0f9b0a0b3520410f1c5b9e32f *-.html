Emilie Goldblum (née Livingston, born January 4, 1983) is a Canadian dancer, aerialist and contortionist, and retired Olympic rhythmic gymnast.[1][2]

Career[edit]

At the age of 10, she moved to Russia (to Novogorsk near Moscow) to train with Russian rhythmic gymnasts and Russian coaches, such as Irina Viner and Lusi Dimitrova.[citation needed]

Livingston is a three-time national champion of Canada in rhythmic gymnastics. She represented Canada at the 1999 Pan American Games in Winnipeg, Manitoba, winning the gold medal as in the individual all-around. She also represented Canada at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, Australia, placing 18th in the individual all-around competition.[3] In 2000, she competed at 167 cm and 38 kg.[4]

In the film Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets, Livingston was a body double for Rihanna performing Bubble’s pole dance scene.[5] She was also a body double for Emma Stone's aerial work in the film La La Land.

Personal life[edit]

Livingston married American actor Jeff Goldblum on November 8, 2014.[6] Their first son, Charlie Ocean, was born on July 4, 2015, and second son, River Joe, was born April 7, 2017.[7]
