Miami street renamed Trayvon Martin Avenue

New York Daily News 10/10/2020 Joseph Wilkinson

A short Miami road will be named Trayvon Martin Avenue after the Black teenager who was killed in 2012.

Martin was a student at Dr. Michael M. Krop Senior High School in Miami when George Zimmerman fatally shot him about 230 miles north in Sanford, Fla. Martin was visiting his father at the time.

The newly named Trayvon Martin Avenue is a 0.3 mile stretch of road next to his old high school.

Miami-Dade County commissioners unanimously approved the resolution to rename the street, the Miami Herald reported. Miami-Dade County owns the road.
