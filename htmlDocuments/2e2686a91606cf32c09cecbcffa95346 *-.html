The first time a new habit is tested #AprilBlogaDay

Spring Break is over. That is sad enough. However I am worried about this habit of writing daily I picked up from #AprilBlogaDay. Can I keep it up?

My Plan:

Write at lunch: I can do this atleast 3/5 days

Write in the AM: maybe on the weekends

Write after the kids are in bed: maybe the remaining days.

My Steps:

Create reminders on my phone and do my best to follow-through!

What do you think? Any words of wisdom from other #AprilBlogaDay participants?
